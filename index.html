About This System

This is a roleplay Federal Election Commission simulation platform. All election data is stored locally in your browser via localStorage. Results are fully simulated and do not represent real elections.

Version: 2.0 Full Build
Storage: localStorage (browser)
Charts: Chart.js 4.4.1

📖 Tutorial & System Guide

1. Campaign Material System

Campaign Points are earned through actions — never entered manually. Click ⚡ Campaign on any candidate to open their campaign manager.

• Posters: +1pt each, max 8 uses, 1-hour cooldown
• Video Ads: +2pt each, max 5 uses, 2-hour cooldown
• Speech: +1pt per 500 characters, max 2000 chars, 1 use, 4-hour cooldown

Formula: Points = (Posters × 1) + (Videos × 2) + floor(SpeechChars ÷ 500)

2. Ballot vs Campaign Weighting

Each race type uses a specific formula:

• Primary: Campaign 60% + Ballot 40%
• General / Governor / Senate: Campaign 50% + Ballot 60%
• House: Campaign 55% + Ballot 55%

Campaign % = candidate's points ÷ total points for the race
Ballot % = candidate's votes ÷ total votes cast
Final Score = (Campaign% × CW) + (Ballot% × BW), then buff/debuff applied

3. Buff / Debuff System

Each candidate can have a percentage modifier — positive for enthusiasm, endorsements, momentum; negative for scandal, controversy, or poor debate performance. Click ✏️ Edit on a candidate and set the Buff/Debuff % field. Applied after the base score is calculated.

4. Estimated Vote-In Slider

Drag the "% Vote Counted" slider on each race:

• 0–50%: Too Early to Call — high volatility
• 50–80%: Leaning — moderate stability
• 80–90%: Likely — mostly stable
• 90–100%: Projected Winner / Final — locked

Randomness only applies below 90%. Large leads won't flip unrealistically.

5. Delegates System (Primary)

In Primary races, delegates are awarded proportionally based on final score:

Delegates = round(FinalScore% × StateDelegates)

Rounding is corrected so totals always sum to the state's delegate count. Set the delegate count on each race card.

6. Discord F.E.C. Copy Format

Every race has a 📋 Copy F.E.C. button. It generates a properly formatted Discord message with the correct emoji IDs used by the F.E.C. roleplay server, ready to paste directly into Discord.

7. Scenario Simulator

Go to Scenario Sim, select any active race, and click Load. The system auto-generates a Scenario B with ±30% vote swings and ±5% buff changes, then shows side-by-side results and flags any winner changes.

8. Results Archive

Click 📁 Archive on any race to permanently save its results. View, search, and compare archived results from the Archive tab. Data persists in localStorage.
